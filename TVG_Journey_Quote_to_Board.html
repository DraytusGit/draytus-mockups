Step 1 of 3
Quotes Page
When a prospective couple completes the WordPress quote builder, their submission lands here automatically. TVG staff use this page to triage new leads as they arrive.
Three dates, three prices Each quote captures up to three preferred dates with a separate estimated total per date. Staff open a quote to see the full breakdown.
Draft vs Finalized Draft means the couple has calculated a quote but not yet confirmed a preferred date. Finalized means a date has been selected and the enquiry formally submitted.
Next step Staff open a quote, review the contact details, and link it to an existing contact or create a new one. The quote then appears on that contact record.

Step 2 of 3
Contact Page
The Contact record is the central hub for a lead. All Quotes submitted by that person appear here, alongside any Events created from those quotes. A contact can have as many of each as needed.
Quotes on the contact When a quote is linked to a contact — by email match or manually — it appears in the Quotes section. Multiple quotes from different venue searches all gather here.
Events of any status When TVG staff promote a quote to an Event, that event appears on the contact. Draft events at any pre-Pencil status — Enquiry, Opportunity, Viewing Booked — are all listed here. Staff can clone and tweak events to compare date options.
Availability is not blocked Pre-Pencil events do not remove a date from the calendar. Only once an event reaches Pencil or Confirmed does the date become unavailable.

Step 3 of 3
Board
The board gives TVG a live pipeline view across all venues. How a card appears depends on where it sits in the journey.
Before Pencil: Contact cards Events in Enquiry, Opportunity, Viewing Booked, or any other pre-Pencil status appear as Contact cards. The card represents the lead being worked, not a committed booking. The contact name is shown, and staff can see how many quotes and draft events sit behind it.
At Pencil and Confirmed: Event cards Once an event is moved to Pencil, the card changes. It becomes an Event card showing the specific booking reference, venue, date, and guest count. The coloured left border signals the status. Confirmed events block that date from availability.
One event per contact can reach Pencil A contact may hold multiple draft events, but only one progresses to Pencil. The remaining drafts stay on the contact record for reference.
