A few quick tips before we head into the first breakout.

01
Introduce yourself first
Start with something personal — where you're based, what you're excited about right now. Relationships start with people, not pitches.
02
Then talk about your business
Keep it clear. What do you do, who do you do it for, and what makes you the right person to call?
03
Finish with your ask
Tell the room exactly how they can help — an introduction, a conversation, a referral. The more specific the ask, the more likely it lands.
04
Watch the clock
There's a timer in the top right of each breakout room. Reminder messages appear as time runs down. Make sure everyone gets their turn.
